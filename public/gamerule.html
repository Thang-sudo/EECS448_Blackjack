BlackJack Rules

  • Welcome to Blackjack, a card game where it's you against the dealer! (Even in multiplayer mode)
  • Each player starts out with 1 card, including the dealer.
  • Your goal is to have the sum of your cards be greater than the dealer's set, without exceeding 21!
  • You have an opportunity to request as many additional cards as you like, as long as you dont pass 21.
  • You also have a chance to bet chips on each round, starting the game with 50 chips.
  • If you can accumulate 100 chips, you win!
