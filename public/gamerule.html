BlackJack Rules

Singleplayer Mode

  • In singleplayer mode, it's you against the dealer!
  • You and the dealer start out with 1 card.
  • Your goal is to have the sum of your cards be greater than the dealer's set, without exceeding 21!
  • You have an opportunity to request (Hit) as many additional cards as you like, as long as you dont pass 21.
  • You also have a chance to bet chips on each round, starting the game with 50 chips.
  • The dealer has the ability to acquire new cards as well, and will do so until the dealer's sum is greater than or equal to 17.
  • If you can accumulate 100 chips, you win!

Multiplayer Mode

  • In multiplayer mode, you have the ability to play against a player from a different web browser.
  • Each player will be competing against a separate dealer, your goal is to reach 100 Chips before the other player!
  • On the flip side, you lose if you run out of chips before your opponent.
  • The separate dealers possess the same intelligence and difficulty as in singleplayer mode.
